Observational Techniques in Astronomy

Curated resources for learning and teaching observational techniques in astronomy.

Resources

  • AST4723 — Public Materials (UF): Upper-level course on astronomical observations; optical and radio spectroscopy and data analysis with Jupyter notebooks.
  • AST3722 — Public Materials: Public repository of course materials and notebooks for observational astronomy.
  • Rice University Observatory: Observatory information, resources, and observing activities at Rice University.
  • PHY217 — Observational Astronomy (Sheffield): Lecture notes, labs, and resources on observational methods.
